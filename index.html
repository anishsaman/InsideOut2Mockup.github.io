New School Year, New Emotions.

Anxiety

She's a bundle of nerves who constantly worries about Riley's wellbeing. Will Riley trip and fall in hockey and be laughed at for the rest of the school year? How will Riley impress the popular girl who wants to be her friend? Anxiety has the best intentions and lives in the future, but her overprotective nature may get in the way of the other emotions!

Envy

Popularity! Cool Hair! The cute boy in math class! Envy wants it all--- even if what she so desperately desires may not be her's. She wants Riley to have the very best of absolutely everything, and knows she can. If other people can have what they want, why wouldn't Riley deserve the same, if not, better!? While admirable, will such desire turn into greed and jealousy?

Ennui

Meh. That's all she's got. Ennui is bored. So very bored. Nothing excites her and she've even more unimpressed by the shenagians of the other emotions. She endlessly scrolls on her phone, and no avail, finds nothing interesting. You can find her in a doctor's waiting room, a math lesson in class, or when Dad starts to blab about work. Good luck trying to crack anything out of her.
